Analysis of Long Decisions

May 1999

Decisions longer then 5 pages

Total number of decisions: 28.

  • Out of which: 20 decisions were rejected, and 8 decisions were accepted (allowed) fully or partially.
  • Only four decisions had a dissent opinion (Justices E. Matza, Y. Kedmi, T. Strasburg-Cohen and H. Ariel).

Average time for decisions: 1.67 years.
