Analysis of Long Decisions

February 1999

Decisions longer then 5 pages

Total number of decisions: 36.

  • Out of which: 20 decisions were rejected, and 16 decisions were accepted (allowed) fully or partially.
  • Only two decisions had a dissent opinion (Justices A. Barak and Y. Kedmi).

Average time for decisions: 1.91 years.
